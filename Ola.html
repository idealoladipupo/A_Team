Total Sales by Product Category & Subcategory

Ola's task sought to visually depict total sales across product categories and sub-categories from 2012 to 2015 using animated bar charts. Each bar represented cumulative sales for a specific product category annually. Interactivity was enhanced by adjusting color opacity upon hovering, providing immediate feedback. Selecting a category bar revealed a pop-up chart detailing sales of individual sub-products. This dynamic visualization enabled deeper insights into sales trends and product performance. Additionally, users can now select specific years to view corresponding sales data, enhancing analytical focus. This feature enriches the user experience, facilitating targeted analysis of sales dynamics.
