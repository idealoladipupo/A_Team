Profit by Top 10 Products

Madonna's task displayed the top 10 products with the highest profit and showed the trend of the sub-categories in four different years. She used a multi-line chart to show the performance of every product for each of the 4 years in the data, 2012, 2013, 2014 and 2015. 2014 was the year that the company made the most profit with Phones. However, Bookcases on the other hand dipped tremendously in 2013.
Her interactivity highlighted selected lines and dimmed the other lines, and she incorporated a tool tip in the chart that showed more information on hovered sections of the line chart like, profit amount, product sub-category and the year. More details on the chart were included, like the legend, chart title etc.
