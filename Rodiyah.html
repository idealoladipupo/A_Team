Total Sales by Region & Countries

Rodiyah's task centered on visualising total sales across regions and countries. Initially, she presented the data using bubble charts, where each bubble represented the total sales generated by a region.
The interactivity was enhanced by adjusting the color opacity when hovering over a bubble, additionally, showing a dark circle around the bubbles when hovered over. Futhermore, when selecting a bubble representing a specific region, a pop-up is revealed showing a donut chart showcasing the distribution of total sales generated by countries within that region. The donut chart included a legend detailing the countries contributing to the total sales within the selected region. The interactivity was enhanced through tooltips, displaying pertinent information like countries % contribution to sales e.t.c, when hovering over a pie in the donut chart.
